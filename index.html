About
This poetic observatory visualizes real lunar phase data through jewelry-like celestial objects. The pearl represents the Moon and the black diamond represents the Sun, while their changing geometry with Earth defines the phase you see now. Use search to switch cities, drag to preview phase progression, and swipe right to inspect the orbital relationship behind each phase.
